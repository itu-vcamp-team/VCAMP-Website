Wedo Social

Algorithmic social discovery platform designed to solve the "Paradox of Choice" in urban nightlife and event planning.

Community First

Wedo flips the traditional event model. Instead of searching for "what to do," users broadcast "who they are" through their preferences and past behavior. Our platform then connects compatible groups of people to curated experiences, fostering genuine real-world connections over passive consumption.

User Experience Design

Frictionless Discovery

The "Vibe Check" interface replaces endless scrolling with a simple tinder-style swipe mechanic for categories (e.g., "Chill Jazz", "Rave", "Art Gallery"). This generates a cold-start profile in under 30 seconds.

Ephemeral Squads

Users can form temporary "Squads" for a specific night. These group chats unlock features like bill splitting, live location sharing, and collective voting on the next venue, automatically archiving 24 hours after the event ends.

Recommendation Engine

Collaborative Filtering

We employ Matrix Factorization (SVD) to identify latent factors in user behavior. By analyzing user-event interactions, we can predict a user's likelihood of enjoying a specific venue even if they have never visited it, based on the patterns of similar users.

Contextual Bandits

To curb feedback loops, we utilize a Multi-Armed Bandit algorithm for exploration. This injects a controlled amount of random, high-variance suggestions to diversify user taste profiles and ensure new venues get exposure.

Technical Infrastructure
