Design Decisions

Wing Configuration

We chose a high-aspect-ratio wing design to maximize flight efficiency and endurance. The wing features a modified airfoil optimized for our expected flight envelope.

Rationale: The high aspect ratio reduces induced drag, allowing for longer flight times with the same battery capacity.

Material Selection

Carbon fiber composite was selected for the primary structure due to its excellent strength-to-weight ratio. EPP foam is used for impact resistance in the fuselage.

Rationale: Weight reduction directly translates to increased flight time and payload capacity.

Autopilot System

Pixhawk 4 was selected for its proven reliability, extensive documentation, and active community support. It provides all necessary sensors and processing power.

Rationale: Open-source platform allows for customization while maintaining reliability.

Power System

4S LiPo battery configuration provides optimal balance between voltage requirements and weight. Battery management system ensures safe operation.

Rationale: 4S configuration provides sufficient voltage for motors while keeping weight manageable.
